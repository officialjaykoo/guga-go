# GUGA Go Rules
- Board size: 19 x 13
- Moves 1 to 4 (including pass) place neutral stones (green): Black → White → Black → White
- A pass in the first 4 moves also counts as a neutral-stone phase move
- A maximum of 4 neutral stones can be placed, positions chosen by players
- During the first 4 moves, capture/self-atari checks are skipped
- From move 5, normal alternating play starts
- Neutral stones are a third stone type and do not connect to black/white groups
- Neutral stones cannot be captured or marked as dead stones
- Neutral stones occupy intersections but do not form territory by themselves
- Neutral stones act like walls; territory can still be formed if surrounded by one side
- Auto dead-stone suggestion is for reference only
- Time setting: 30 seconds, 3 lives
- Undo request: up to 3 times per player per game
- Score request: available after move 100, only on your turn
- Score request auto-accepts if no response within 15 seconds
- Komi: 0
